Odeu-Ministries:

we seek to reach and challenge those who shape the
ideas of culture with credibility and the beauty
of the gospel of Jesus Christ to questions of the
skeptic.

As believers in Jesus Christ the ONLY BEGOTTEN SON of
the Father in heaven, we offer an apologetic–we give
an answer–to the counter claims of secularism and
other worldviews. We address the intellectual and
existential assumptions of our culture, realizing that
for some, the door to the heart is through the mind,
and for others through the imagination.

OUR VISION ;
is folded into four:
  1. Evangelism - 2Timothy 4:5
  2. Apologetics -1peter 1-5
  3. Training - Mathew 28:18-19
  4. Charity work, to help former Muslims who have been
    injured, aboundoned and rejected after giving up everything for
    JESUS CHRIST the only SAVIOUR.

Proverbs 19:17 He that hath pity upon the poor lendeth unto the LORD;
and that which he hath given will he pay him again.

One of the great losses of our time is critical thinking and
Odeu-Ministries is committed to reaching this generation around the world.
We seek to remove the barriers to the cross for the skeptic and
we prepare Christians to give a reason for the hope within.
If you would like to be part of this effort, consider whether
your skills and calling match our calling.
