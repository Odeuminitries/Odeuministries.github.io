Musitafas Story:

I am Mustafa, and I am a Christian. Formerly I was a Muslim,
from a devoted Muslim family. I became a Christian in 2018,
after I was convicted of my sin, and heard the truth of the Gospel
of Jesus Christ. Preachers who came from Odeu Ministries in
Africa(Uganda) were the ones who introduced me to Christ.

My Agony of Pain:
After I confessed Christ as Lord and Savior, I tried to hide the
fact of being a Christian from my relatives,family and clan.
After months I began sharing the gospel to my Mama and siblings,
and they later accepted Jesus Christ as well. However, I didn't know
what would happen next if my Father and clan elders knew I had
converted to Christianity, and had also convinced my Mother and siblings to convert.

In the 9th month, my Dad learned that I and my Mother had converted
from Islam. My Dad, Mr.****, had me beaten nearly to death. Afterwards,
I was thrown into my room in my Father's house and left me there for somedays
without food or drinking water. After days had passed, my Father thought I
would renounce Christ, but my faith had grown stronger. I was very weak
and couldn’t even talk; but, when they asked me to renounce Jesus I refused “No!”
. My Father became furious, as did my Uncle and the Clan leaders.
I was persecuted,tortured and afflicted beaten severely almost to death,and what pains
me is,this was was done while claiming the name of Allah(Muslim God).

My Mama saw my agony and tried to rescue me by called neighbors,and later the police came,
which didn’t help much because my Father all the relatives involved escaped,my
Father and Uncle hid themselves for several months.my case didn't go to the courts of law.

Odeu Ministries and friends funded and they took me to the hospital. During that time My
face had been severely wounded,I lost my left eye which was removed in the
hospital because it had been damaged and was beyond cure.as shown in one of the photos.

I have asthma, and just the memories of this horrible experience trigger attacks.
These attacks are so bad I have been hospitalized for them several times.

After I was hospitalized for my first eye surgery operation and
treating the severely damaged face in that healing process,Father returned
home after hiding several months, my Mother and my siblings were
persecuted afresh,he was calling us KAFROON (meaning dis-believers or
lost soul),In all this my mama refused to denounce and renounce Jesus.
The clan elders tried to force them to denounce and renounce, but they wouldn’t do it.
Finally my father threw us a way from his house.

Musitafas Habitation today:
We moved from northern Uganda, to Ugandas central region,it's along
distance away from my father's region,for security purposes.
We took up residence in a dilapidated and utterly wretched house because we have nowhere else to go.

Musitafa Healthy condition today!.
IAM disabled now work but I try to go and work as a cleaner at an
Indian store, and earning 3000 Ugandan shillings (equivalent to $0.85) a day.
I can no longer attend school, and neither can my siblings.
My siblings have not been able to go to school for the past one year.Odeu Ministries
( Food relief Department) has been helping us to get barely enough food and water to survive
and medication for the nursing of my left Eye which was damaged.
